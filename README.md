# Excess Air – Luggage Space Sharing Platform

## Overview
Excess Air is a concept-based project that allows travelers to monetize unused luggage space by connecting them with people who want to send items to the same destination. It works as a peer-to-peer platform where users can buy or sell extra luggage capacity.

## Problem
Many travelers carry unused luggage space, while others struggle with high shipping costs for small items. There is no simple and affordable way to utilize this unused capacity.

## Solution
Excess Air bridges this gap by enabling travelers to list their available luggage space and allowing others to book it for sending items. This creates a cost-effective and efficient alternative to traditional shipping.

## Features
- List available luggage space  
- Search travelers by destination  
- Connect buyers and travelers  
- Cost sharing for luggage space  

## Use Case
A traveler flying from Ahmedabad to London has extra luggage space. Another user wants to send items to London. Through the platform, they connect and share the luggage space at a lower cost.
